Thai Chicken Lettuce Wraps

Tags:

  • Chicken
  • Soy
  • Paleo
Pg 94 Paleo Perfected: America's Test Kitchen

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless skinless chicken thighs, trimmed into 1-inch pieces
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 4 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 2.5 teaspoons tapioca flour
  • 0.5 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 0.5 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 0.5 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 orange bell pepper
  • 2 shallots
  • 1 head Bibb lettuce

Preparation

Prep Chicken

  1. Spread chicken on a large plate and freeze until firm (~25 minutes).
  2. Pulse half of the chicken in a food processor until finely ground into 1/4 - 1/8 inch pieces (6-10 pulses).
  3. Repeat with the second half of the chicken.

Prep Sauce for Marinade

  1. Whisk together the following:
    • 2 teaspoons fish sauce
    • 1 teaspoon lime juice
    • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
    • 2 teaspoons tapioca flour
    • 0.5 teaspoon red pepper flakes
    • 0.5 teaspoon salt
    • 0.5 teaspoon pepper
  2. Toss chicken in sauce and let marinate for 15 minutes.

Cooking Instructions

  1. Whisk together:
    • 2 tablespoons honey
    • 4 teaspoons fish sauce
    • 5 teaspoons lime juice
    • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
    • 0.5 teaspoon tapioca flour
  2. Heat oil in a 12-inch pan.
  3. Sauté peppers.
  4. Sauté shallots.
  5. Add chicken and cook until no longer pink.
  6. Add the remaining sauce and cook for a few minutes.
